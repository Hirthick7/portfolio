MedClinic AI

Clinical Support System Powered by Artificial Intelligence

Flask Python MongoDB AI/ML Gemini API Healthcare
Live Demo View Code

Problem Statement

Healthcare professionals often face challenges in quickly accessing relevant clinical information, analyzing patient symptoms, and making informed decisions. Traditional systems lack intelligent support and real-time assistance, leading to delays and potential oversights in patient care.

Solution

MedClinic AI is an intelligent clinical support system that leverages artificial intelligence to provide real-time symptom analysis, diagnostic suggestions, and treatment recommendations. The platform uses advanced AI models to analyze patient data and offer evidence-based clinical insights, helping healthcare providers make faster, more informed decisions.

Key Features

AI-Powered Symptom Analysis

Advanced AI algorithms analyze patient symptoms and provide accurate diagnostic suggestions

Treatment Recommendations

Evidence-based medication suggestions and home remedies for common conditions

Patient Data Management

Secure MongoDB database for storing and managing patient records and consultations

Emergency Alerts

Intelligent system to identify critical symptoms and trigger emergency protocols

Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive analytics and insights on patient trends and common conditions

Responsive Design

Fully responsive interface accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Technology Stack

Backend: Python Flask
Database: MongoDB
AI: Gemini 2.0 Flash
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
